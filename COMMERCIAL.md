# AI Company OS — Commercial Strategy

> **Phase:** Pre-v1.0 Development  
> **Current State:** Source-Visible Evidence Repository  
> **Target:** Open Core after v1.0 Productized Alpha

---

## The Model: Open Core + Commercial Layer

AI Company OS is designed as an **open-core platform**:

```
🧊 Open Source Core (Public)
   Engine, Work Order, Skill Registry,
   Launch Pipeline basics, documentation
   ── freely available, community-contributable

💰 Commercial Layer (Paid)
   Premium Skills, industry templates,
   hosted SaaS, enterprise deployment,
   operating kits, support & consulting
```

The open core exists to maximize distribution, trust, and community input.
The commercial layer exists because that's where the real value lives.

---

## Commercial Assets (Not Public)

The following are **not** included in this public repository and will be offered as commercial products:

### 1. Premium Skills Library
- Industry-specific skills (cross-border e-commerce, SaaS, content products)
- High-quality prompts and SOPs
- Validated business workflows

### 2. AI Company OS Operating Kit
- Methodology playbooks and templates
- Launch Pipeline blueprints
- Decision frameworks and Opportunity Card templates
- Case studies from real validation projects

### 3. Hosted SaaS (ai-company-os.com)
- Multi-tenant deployment
- Team collaboration and permissions
- Managed runtime infrastructure
- Usage analytics and dashboards

### 4. Enterprise Deployment
- Private cloud / on-premises installation
- Custom skill development
- Dedicated runtime connectors
- SLA-backed support

### 5. Consulting & Training
- AI-native company setup workshops
- Operating system migration consulting
- Custom product line agent design

- **AGPL v3** — strong copyleft, prevents SaaS cloning

## Why This Strategy

1. **Skills are the moat** — not the 1,900 lines of engine code. Competitors can fork the core engine, but they cannot replicate years of industry-specific skill engineering.
2. **Trust sells** — open source / source-visible = lower adoption friction for enterprise buyers.
3. **Community feeds the commercial layer** — skills contributed by the community increase the platform's value, which increases willingness to pay for enterprise features.
4. **Evidence-based marketing** — every release, every validation project, every real deployment is a case study. The public repo is the sales deck.
