## Learned Patterns

These patterns come from actual system runs rather than abstract design.

### What works
- lead-dispatched multi-agent chains
- checkpoint-based resume for long tasks
- daily heartbeat for autonomous operation

### What does not work well yet
- pure agent-to-agent execution without a lead
- long tasks without checkpoints
- manual dispatch as a bottleneck

## Why these assets matter

These knowledge assets show that AI Company OS is not only producing work.

It is also learning from execution and preserving that learning in reusable form.

This helps the system:

- identify better opportunities
- retain validated workflows
- reuse lessons across projects
- improve future operating decisions
- turn repeated execution into compounding organizational knowledge

AI accumulates knowledge, not just outputs.
